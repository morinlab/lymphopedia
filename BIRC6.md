## Overview

BIRC6, as a negative regulator of non-canonical NF-ÎºB signaling, is implicated in lymphomagenesis. Mutations in the BIRC6 have been found in DLBCL and grey zone lymphoma (GZL).[@sarkozyMutationalLandscapeGray2021],[@reddyGeneticFunctionalDrivers2017]
