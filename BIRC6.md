# BIRC6

## Overview
BIRC6, as a negative regulator of non-canonical NF-ÎºB signaling, is implicated in lymphomagenesis. Mutations in the BIRC6 have been found in DLBCL and grey zone lymphoma (GZL).<sup>1,2</sup>
