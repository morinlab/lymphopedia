# P2RY8
P2RY8 encodes a G proteinâ€“coupled receptor that is expressed on germinal center B cells. Signals through this receptor promote confinement of B cells to the GC niche.<sup>1</sup> Downstream signaling through Galpha13 (encoded by GNA13) and S1PR2 is distrupted by mutations of one of these genes in GC lymphomas including the GCB subgroup of DLBCL<sup>2</sup> and BL.<sup>1</sup>
