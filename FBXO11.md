# FBXO11
## Overview
Somatic mutations in FBXO11 are common in BL<sup>1,2</sup> and appear in a small number of DLBCLs.<sup>3</sup>

|Entity|Tier|Description               |
|:------:|:----:|--------------------------|
|BL    |1   |high-confidence BL gene   |
|DLBCL |1   |high-confidence DLBCL gene|
